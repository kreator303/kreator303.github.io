Collaborate - External Post Production Supervisor

Clients needed a way to receive assets and complete movies / TV episodes without giving external vendors network or Digital Asset Management system access. The dashboard provides the external (vendor) post production supervisor with visibility across all Jobs. The details page provides relevant job details and the system enforces metadata entry standards for the DAM.

  • Roles: UX, Production Artist, Product Management
  • Users: External Post Production Supervisors
  • Clients: Amazon Video, Warner Brothers
  • Date: Summer 2016
  • Agency: Levels Beyond
