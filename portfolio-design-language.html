Levels Beyond Design Language and Interaction Specifications

I inherited a disparate suite of applications and created a unified design language that standardized the interaction paradigms across all applications regardless of if they lived on the desktop, in a browser, or inside Adobe Creative Suite Applications. I worked with a creative director to flesh out the standard color palettes, iconography, and control interactions. I created a UX Wiki as a repository for this knowledge. The wiki also served as a training resource for the UX team members and as a link target for engineering JIRAs to ensure that stories always referenced the latest UX specifications.
