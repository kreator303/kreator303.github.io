Collaborate - Internal Post Production Supervisor

Clients needed a way to receive assets and complete movies / TV episodes without giving external vendors network or Digital Asset Management system access. The dashboard provides the internal post production supervisor with visibility across all Productions across all external vendors. The details page provides relevant job details and the system enforces metadata entry standards for the DAM.

  • Roles: UX, Production Artist, Product Management
  • Users: Internal Post Production Supervisors
  • Clients: Amazon Video, Warner Brothers
  • Date: Fall 2016
  • Agency: Levels Beyond
